BigBlockIfTrue

Maintainer and Developer

BigBlockIfTrue has been following Bitcoin Cash development since the inception of the project in 2017, actively participating in various related community discussions over the years. He previously created one of the first graphics packs for Bitcoin Cash (https://bitcoinlogo.eu) and translated Electron Cash into Dutch, and now contributes to the development of the Bitcoin Cash Node source code.

BigBlockIfTrue holds a PhD degree in an engineering discipline and has professional programming experience in various languages, including C++, Python, and Java.
